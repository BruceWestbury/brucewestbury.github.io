Research

My interests include representation theory, tensor categories, quantum groups, diagrammatic algebra, and combinatorial structures arising from Lie theory.

Current work focuses on trivalent graph computations related to the F₄ and E₆ series, including reduction systems, closed graph evaluations, and computational approaches to polynomial obstructions.
