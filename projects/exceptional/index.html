Exceptional series through the fifth tensor power

These tables describe structure common to the tensor powers of the adjoint representation through degree five. Generic labels, branching rules, products, quantum dimensions, and polynomial functors are kept separate from the characters at the eight ordinary points of the series.

The eight points

Type \(A_1\) \(A_2\) \(G_2\) \(D_4\) \(F_4\) \(E_6\) \(E_7\) \(E_8\)
\(n\) \(1/3\) \(1/2\) \(2/3\) \(1\) \(3/2\) \(2\) \(3\) \(5\)

The intermediate point at \(n=4\) is not included because its character data has not been determined.

Generic structure

  • Labels and Casimirs
  • Branching and partner matrices
  • Product decompositions
  • Quantum dimensions
  • Schur functors
  • Orthogonal functors

Characters

The character tables record the signed character of the identity component for every label and ordinary type.
