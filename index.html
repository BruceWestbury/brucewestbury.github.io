Bruce Westbury

My research sits at the intersection of mathematics, computation, and diagrammatic algebra. I am interested in the combinatorial and representation-theoretic structures that arise from trivalent graphs, with a particular focus on the exceptional Lie groups and their associated spider categories.

Current projects explore the F₄ series and E₆ series of representations through the lens of closed trivalent graph computations and polynomial obstructions. The goal is to give explicit, computer-assisted proofs of relations in these categories by enumerating closed graphs and identifying the invariants that certify irreducibility or vanishing.
