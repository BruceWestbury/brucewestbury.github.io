About

I am a mathematician working on diagrammatic algebra, representation theory, and combinatorics, with a particular interest in the exceptional Lie groups and their spider categories. This site collects research notes, computations, and publications arising from projects on the F₄ and E₆ series. The project pages are intended to be readable alongside the corresponding papers, with enough detail to reproduce the computations.
