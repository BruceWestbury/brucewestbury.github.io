# About

-I was an undergraduate student at Peterhouse, University of Cambridge and graduated in 1979.
-My Ph. D. supervisor was Roger Plymen at the University of Manchester.
-I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Warwick where I worked with John Jones.
